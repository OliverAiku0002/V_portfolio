Climbing System



Third-Person Wall Climbing System developed in Unreal Engine 5 using C++. It provides robust climbing mechanics for third-person games, enabling seamless interactions with walls, ledges, and other climbable objects.

Features
Wall Climbing: The player can climb walls by interacting with climbable surfaces.

Automatic Top Transition: The system smoothly transitions the player to the top of a wall upon reaching the edge.

Climbing Down: Allows players to climb down ledges with smooth animations and controls.
