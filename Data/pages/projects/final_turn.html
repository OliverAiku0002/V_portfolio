Final Turn



Final Turn is a tense, turn-based top-down puzzle-stealth adventure game built in Unreal Engine 5.4 for Android.

Navigate using node-based movement. Solve pathing puzzles by planning each move carefully. Avoid detection, stealth-kill guards, or destroy obstacles. Strategically escape or clear your way through each level.
